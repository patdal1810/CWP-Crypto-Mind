## What is OHLCV?

OHLCV means:

- Open: price at the beginning of the candle
- High: highest price during the candle
- Low: lowest price during the candle
- Close: price at the end of the candle
- Volume: how much was traded

## What is a Candle?

A candle represents price movement within a timeframe.

Example:

If you choose 1H, each candle represents one hour.
